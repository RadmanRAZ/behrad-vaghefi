The negative middle space represents the mother`s womb, reminiscent of a familiar space for children, the space we have all been in over a period of time and in that closed environment we have completed our growth and development, an atmosphere full of warm relaxation from the mother`s body. An alley passes through the selected site , which turns it into a street and the volume of the bean is placed in front of it. Statement Can technology pave the way for creativity? Can modern tools ,methods and new designing software, machines and digital making machines, pave the way for creative projects and become the main factor of creativity? this is the question that have been on the minds of many designers for a long time although at first it seemed that the answer to all these questions was no and also it seemed that the answer was yes! ,but these yes and no, aren’t the best and convincing answer for these questions and it seems like basic research and laboratory experiments can help better understanding this subject considering the multitude of creative projects of the contemporary world, working with bricks and examples of robotic bricks layouts in a especial way, it seems that most of the creativity may have been done with these attractive traditional materials. But we hope that technology give us a chance to create newer methods. For this reason, in this experimental training program, we return to the basics. We test the basic principles and observe the outcome of submitting very minor changes. Simple rules like , displacement and rotation , will be our focus. one degree of rotation and one millimeter of displacement is important for this project. Creative designing can take shape with these basics and advancing them to our new brick-laying systems The combination of traditional manufacturing methods and digital tools has always fascinated me(how the distance between machine and human, computer and hand is filed) Considering the problem of this research program again, it will raise these themes once again… how can we turn digital fluid data into rigid brick products while we do not use robots to pick them?... what is the path of converting design digital data to manufacturing data? These are the main challenge of this training program. where those millimeters and degrees, must be converted into a device that can be made and be a guarantee of the objective reality of the project This relationship between digital data and physical construction and development of integrated manual and machine systems and in the end , development of adaptive manufacturing methods , are the issues which is shared and experienced in this workshop
